About SEEE – GVPCE(A)

About SEEE Club

The Society of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (SEEE) is the official student club of the Electrical and Electronics Engineering department at GVP College of Engineering (Autonomous). SEEE club was started in 1997. Our club encourages not only curricular activities, but also all types of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities like sports, cultural programs, technical events and many more.

Our Vision

To create technically strong, creative and responsible electrical engineers who are ready for industry, research and societal needs.

Our Mission

  • To conduct technical events, workshops and hands-on sessions for students.
  • To encourage participation in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities.
  • To build leadership, teamwork and communication skills.
  • To connect students with industry experts, alumni and professionals.
