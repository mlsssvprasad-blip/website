GVP College of Engineering (Autonomous)

About the College

Gayatri Vidya Parishad College of Engineering (Autonomous), Visakhapatnam, established in 1996, is one of the leading engineering institutions in Andhra Pradesh. Affiliated to Andhra University, the college is known for quality education, strong faculty, advanced laboratories, research culture, and excellent student placements.

Mission of the College

To impart high-quality technical education, promote innovation and research, and develop ethically strong professionals who contribute to society and national development.
